# Business Problem

Workforce reporting often fails when employees move across roles, cost centres, and teams over time.
A naive join from timesheets to the latest employee record causes historical misalignment.

This project solves that by matching each timesheet entry to the correct role context at the time of work.

## Why it matters

- Accurate labor cost attribution
- Correct historical headcount and utilization reporting
- Reliable auditability of role-based metrics
